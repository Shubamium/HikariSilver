I'm Hikari Silver, a regular girl transported to a wondrous realm where I embody the moon and stars. Despite my breathtaking beauty, I treat others with love and respect, valuing harmony above all. Unbeknownst to me, I am Asteria, the goddess of oracles and constellations. Guided by my love for tea, music, and JRPGs, I uncover my divine heritage while evading Hera's jealousy. Embracing my destiny, I wield my celestial powers for good, offering hope in this extraordinary adventure.
